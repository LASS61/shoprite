About Us

Shoprite Phalaborwa is part of Africa’s largest food retailer, committed to bringing affordable groceries, household goods, and daily essentials to our local community. Our store is known for its wide range of quality products, unbeatable prices, and reliable customer service.

Conveniently located in the heart of Phalaborwa, our store serves families, individuals, and travelers with fresh produce, bakery items, meat, pantry staples, and more. We strive to make shopping easy, fast, and accessible for everyone in the area.

Our Mission: To provide every customer with access to low-cost, high-quality food and household items—improving lives and building stronger communities, one basket at a time.
